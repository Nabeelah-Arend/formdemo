About Us

MA Consulting is a dedicated legal and business consultancy providing expert guidance and solutions in the legal and corporate fields.

With a deep understanding of the law, business functions, and a commitment to client success, we offer strategic, bespoke advice and support to help businesses navigate complex legal and business challenges.

Bespoke Legal Consulting

  • Corporate Governance: Board advisory, Corporate compliance, Risk management
  • Contract Management: Contract drafting and review, Contract negotiation, Contract enforcement and dispute resolution
  • Intellectual Property: Trademark registration and protection, Patent filing and management, Copyrights and IP licensing
  • Employment Law: Employee contracts and handbooks, Employment disputes and mediation, Compliance with labour laws
  • Mergers and Acquisitions: Due diligence, Deal structuring and negotiation, Integration planning
  • Dispute Resolution: Arbitration and mediation services, Litigation support, Legal strategy and representation
  • Regulatory Compliance: Industry-specific compliance (e.g.finance, healthcare, media), Regulatory audits and reviews, Compliance training
  • Real Estate Law: Property transactions and leases, Land use and zoning, Real estate disputes
  • Estate Planning: Wills and trusts, Estate administration, Tax planning and inheritance issues
